CATEGORIES

Biko

MAIN INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 cup canned coconut milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 pieces of pandan leaf

    Latik Syrup

  • 2 cups canned coconut milk
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • Vegetable or coconut oil (for pan grease)

    Steps:

  • Grease a baking pan (preferabbly 8x8) and set aside.
  • Add the rice, coconut milk, cup of water and 2pc pandan leaves in a large pot over medium heat, mix the milk and water. Add the rice and leaves.
  • Let it cook until all liquids are absorbed. The rice should be almost cooked by now.
  • While waiting, prepare the latik by combining the 2 cups coocnut milk and the 1 cup dark brown sugar. Cook over medium heat while constantly stirring. Cook until the liquid becomes dark syrup.
