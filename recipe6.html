Buko Juice

MAIN INGREDIENTS: STEPS:
Juice from 3 fresh, young coconuts Open coconuts on the top to create a hole to extract the juice.
Coconut meat from the 3 coconuts Add condensed milk into the pitcher then mix until totally dissolved.
3 tbsp condensed milk Open the coconuts in half, and using a fork, score the young coconut meat before removing it with a spoon, place the coconut meat in the coconut water mixture, place ice cubes and done.
