Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is the classic Filipino peanut stew filled with slices of oxtail, beef tripe, and an abundance of vegetables like long green beans, eggplants, Chinese cabbage, and banana heart simmered in a rich, thick, nutty sauce.

Arroz Caldo

Arroz Caldo is a savory, flavorful Filipino rice porridge with chicken and a crispy garlic topping. This food is similar to that of American chicken and rice soup, but with crispy meat and garlic chips.
