Biscocho

MAIN INGREDIENTS:

  • Day-old pandesal or bread
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Garlic/butter spread

    STEPS

  • Just coat the bread with a sweet mixture of sugar, butter and a spread of garlic and butter.
  • Bake it a second time.
