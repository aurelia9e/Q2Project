About Us

Gourmet Home Eats is an established solo-run shop that not only sells food, but also puts recipes online for others to see!

Established during the height of 2020 originating as a community pantry, Gourmet Home Eats had humble beginnings in helping the needy with their food and eventually, in 2022, opened up a proper store.
