Guyabano Juice

MAIN INGREDIENTS: STEPS:
Guyabano Wash the guyabano under cold running water. Peel the skin off and cut the fruit in half, remove the middle rind. Remove the seeds from the flesh and discard them. Combine the seeded flesh and part of the water in a blender. Process until proceed.
Sugar In a fine mesh strainer, strain the blended fruit and the remaining water to remove fruit fibers.
Lime juice (prevents oxidation) Add sugar and lime juice. Stir until blended and put in the ref to chill.
