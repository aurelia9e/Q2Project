Buko Pandan

    "A sweet coconut drink made with pandan jelly and nata de coco."

MAIN INGREDIENTS:

  • Buko
  • Green jelly powder or agar/gulaman- use unflavored jelly powder.
  • Pandan Flavor Extract
  • Coconut water or buko juice
  • Cream
  • Condensed milk

    Steps:

  • Make the pandan jelly.
  • Prepare the other ingredients.
  • Combine all ingredients together in a mixing bowl.Place in a refrigerator/freezer.
